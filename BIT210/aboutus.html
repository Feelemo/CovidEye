About Us

Our Company

Launch at 2020 , our aims is to develop a full-functional website to enable the health ministry to keep track and administer the tests from the various test centres and provide an online platform that enables the test centre managers, testers, and patients to use the functionalities provided and perform their jobs efficiently



Our Objectives

  • Provide a centralized user-friendly web application (website) and responsive user interface that allows target users to interact with the system.

  • Facilitate coordination and consistency in administering and keeping track of every single test conducted and the number of test kit stocks into the system systematically

  • Enhance the overall job efficiency by centralizing all results into one system for easy to track, review and update for the testers, and managing the availability of test kit stocks for the test centre managers



Some Advice

Sanitize your hands regularly

Regularly and thoroughly clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash with soap and water that can kill viruses that may be on your hands.

Use face mask at all times

The general public is advised to wear a fabric mask if there is widespread community transmission, and especially where physical distancing cannot be maintained.

Seek medical assistance

If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention, but call by telephone in advance if possible and follow the directions of your local health authority.
